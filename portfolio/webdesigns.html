Shadows Food & Fun

This school project offered us a unique opportunity to delve into the world of local cuisine by selecting a restaurant for a comprehensive revamp. After careful consideration, I chose a beloved local eatery as my subject. Armed with my creative tools, I embarked on a journey to transform its online presence. Utilizing Adobe XD, I meticulously crafted mockups envisioning a sleek and user-friendly website design. Drawing upon my expertise in HTML, CSS, and Javascript, I breathed life into these designs, coding a dynamic website from scratch. However, the project didn't stop at web development; it extended to brand identity too. Leveraging Adobe's suite of design software, I conceptualized and crafted a captivating logo, seamlessly integrating it into the website to reflect the restaurant's renewed image. This project not only honed my technical skills but also nurtured my creativity and ability to bring visions to life in the digital realm.

Hard Skills Used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Adobe XD, Figma, Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, and Graphic Design
