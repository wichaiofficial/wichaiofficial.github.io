Project Overview

The project is developed within the ASP.NET framework, leveraging its robust features and functionalities. It's not just another forum site; rather, it serves as a vibrant hub tailored specifically for gamers to congregate, interact, and collaborate with like-minded individuals. Imagine a dynamic amalgamation of Reddit's community-driven discussions but tailored exclusively for gaming enthusiasts, offering a plethora of engaging features and activities. Users can delve into discussions ranging from game strategies, troubleshooting, to sharing memorable gaming moments. Beyond discussions, the platform fosters a sense of community by enabling users to organize and participate in gaming events, whether it's rallying fellow players for exhilarating raids or embarking on epic adventures together. Moreover, it serves as a platform for hosting thrilling gaming tournaments, igniting friendly competition and showcasing gaming prowess. In essence, it's more than just a forum; it's a vibrant ecosystem where gamers unite, collaborate, and celebrate their passion for gaming.

Hard Skills Used: HTML, CSS, C#, Azure DevOps, Agile Development Process, Visual Studios, Github, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, and Graphic Design
